Tanzania's Iconic Wildlife

Discover the magnificent animals you'll encounter on your Tanzanian safari adventure

African Lion

The majestic king of the savannah, African lions are social cats that live in prides. Witness their power and grace during your safari in Serengeti and Ngorongoro.

Carnivore Social Pride Nocturnal Hunter
See Lion Safaris

Cheetah

The fastest land animal, cheetahs are built for speed. Watch these elegant predators in action across the Serengeti plains, reaching speeds up to 70 mph.

Fastest Animal Diurnal Hunter Solitary
See Cheetah Safaris

African Elephant

The largest land animal on Earth, African elephants are intelligent, social creatures that form strong family bonds in Tarangire and Serengeti National Parks.

Largest Land Animal Highly Social Herbivore
See Elephant Safaris

Black Rhino

Critically endangered black rhinos can be spotted in Ngorongoro Crater. These solitary browsers are a rare and precious sight on any Tanzanian safari.

Endangered Solitary Browser
See Rhino Safaris

Hippopotamus

Despite their bulky appearance, hippos are surprisingly agile in water. Observe them in rivers and lakes during your safari adventure in Selous and Ruaha.

Semi-aquatic Territorial Herbivore
See Hippo Safaris

Masai Giraffe

The world's tallest land animal, giraffes roam the savannahs of Tanzania. Their distinctive pattern and graceful movements are unforgettable sights.

Tallest Animal Social Browser

Must-Visit Tanzanian Destinations

Explore Tanzania's most spectacular national parks and conservation areas

Serengeti National Park

Home to the Great Migration, where millions of wildebeest and zebras traverse the plains. Experience unparalleled wildlife viewing in Africa's most famous safari destination.

  • Great Migration
  • Big Five Spotting
  • Hot Air Balloon Safaris
  • Year-round Game Viewing
Book Serengeti Safari

Ngorongoro Conservation Area

The world's largest intact volcanic caldera, home to an incredible density of wildlife including the Big Five. Often called "Africa's Garden of Eden."

  • World's Largest Caldera
  • High Wildlife Density
  • Big Five in One Place
  • Maasai Cultural Experiences
Book Ngorongoro Safari

Tarangire National Park

Famous for its large elephant herds and ancient baobab trees. During dry season, Tarangire has one of the highest concentrations of wildlife in Tanzania.

  • Large Elephant Herds
  • Ancient Baobab Trees
  • Bird Watching Paradise
  • Seasonal Wildlife Concentration
Book Tarangire Safari

Mount Kilimanjaro

Africa's highest peak and the world's tallest free-standing mountain. Challenge yourself with a trek to the Roof of Africa through diverse ecological zones.

  • Africa's Highest Peak
  • Multiple Climbing Routes
  • Diverse Ecosystems
  • UNESCO World Heritage Site
Book Kilimanjaro Trek

Zanzibar Island

The Spice Island with pristine beaches, rich history, and vibrant culture. Perfect combination with safari for relaxation and cultural immersion.

  • Pristine White Sand Beaches
  • Stone Town UNESCO Site
  • Spice Tours
  • Snorkeling & Diving
Book Zanzibar Trip

Lake Manyara National Park

Known for its tree-climbing lions and flamingo-filled alkaline lake. A compact park with surprising diversity located at the base of the Great Rift Valley.

  • Tree-Climbing Lions
  • Flamingo Populations
  • Groundwater Forest
  • Great Rift Valley Views
Book Manyara Safari
